// about me

Analyst by trade.
Engineer by ambition.

I started as a Software Developer at Citi in Chennai, building trade processing systems for a cross-functional team of 15. I moved into data, spending the last 4+ years at Banner Health deep in revenue cycle analytics — SQL, Power BI, Tableau, SSIS, Azure Data Lake.

I've designed dashboards consumed by C-suite, led migration of 300+ reports across platforms, built OLAP tables in PostgreSQL on Azure, and written backend SQL that powers multi-million dollar revenue cycle decisions across multiple healthcare verticals.

Now I'm making the next leap — from BI analyst to full-stack data engineer. I'm building hands-on with Python, the Anthropic API, and Databricks. My goal is a role where I design the pipelines, not just the dashboards.

I also have a published research paper from my internship at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) on driver vision and road safety — the project where my love for messy, real-world data began.
